About Us

The campus of Sai College of Education, Jadla (Nawanshahr) is on the main road (National Highway) from Chandigarh to Jalandhar via Roop Nagar about 10 Kms from Nawanshahr. The campus is in a pollution-free environment away from crowded city life offering a congenial atmosphere for all-round growth of the budding teachers.

Vision of the College

To prepare and develop competent, innovative, and farsighted teachers who can meet the requirements of the global competitive world and contribute to academic excellence.

To provide a value-based curriculum and dynamic academic environment for strengthening faith in humanistic, social and moral values as well as in Indian cultural heritage and democracy.

To create facilities for imparting quality education and grow into a center of excellence in the field of teacher education.

Mission of the College

To imbibe in our prospective teachers Dedication, Diligence, Sense of Discrimination, and Dignity of the teaching profession.
