 Welcome to Sai College of Education Jadla (Nawanshahr)

“SAI COLLEGE OF EDUCATION, JADLA (NAWANSHAHR)” is on the main National Highway from Chandigarh to Jalandhar near Nawanshahr. This is a B.Ed. Co-Education college with intial strength of 100 students. This college is affiliated to Guru Nanak Dev University, Amritsar & approved by N.C.T.E. (National Council for Teacher Education, Jaipur. a statuoury body formed under the act by the Parliament). The institution has sufficient accomodation with vast open space and latest devices to bring competance in Teaching process. To mention a few: Micro-Teaching (Communication) lab, a unique feature added to the college working scheme. This is specially designed and fitted with automatic cameras with the facility to record and reproduce to be evaluated by the indiviual student or the examiner. Library system is Partially-Automated.
