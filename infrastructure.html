Features

Apart form providing Education the College inculcates a sense of integrity, efficiency and feeling of co-operativeness and above all confidence.

In order to motivate the students to inculcate competitive spirit in them, College encourages the co-curricular and extra curricular activities through inter-house competitions in Art, Sports. Competitions are held in music, debates, current affair discussions. Education is an instrument that brings about change, a positive change to be precise. It should liberate an individual from dysfunctional social and environmental conditions and to help one programmed in a holistic way.

Personel Development program: To develop leadership traits and qualities, students are encouraged to be a part of various committees � like Discipline, Cultural, NSS, Beautification, Various clubs which function under the guidance of faculty members. Development of Communication Skills: Group discussions, debates, declamations, quizzes, media, situation appreciation, projects and essay writing are organized on for improving the expression and to strengthen the confidence and communication skills of the students.

 
 

Sports

The College encourages the students for sports. An inter-house competition is conducted and winners are awarded with prizes. Apart from this a large number of Athletic items namely discuss throws, javelin, shot put etc for both boys and girls are also organized. There are a variety of indoor and outdoor games like Table Tennis, Badminton, Cricket, Volleyball, Basket Ball, Kho-Kho and athletics / throws etc.

 

Gymnasium

College maintains a standard GYMNASIUM catering to the needs of interested students and GYM coach (D.P.E.) is there to guide and instruct them.

 

Transport

Sai College of Education, Jadla (Nawanshahr) is on National Highway from Chandigarh to Jalandhar. Bus services around the clock are avaliable. Buses drop and pickup the students from the Gate of the college.
